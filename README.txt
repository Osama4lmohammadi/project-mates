## 📝 Description

University students frequently encounter significant challenges when trying to find ideal partners for their graduation projects[cite: 1]. Traditional methods rely heavily on random social circles, which often leads to skill imbalances within teams or interpersonal conflicts due to diverging work ethics[cite: 1].  

**Project Mates** delivers a systematic, data-driven solution to streamline this process[cite: 1]. Students begin by creating a comprehensive profile and completing a detailed questionnaire that maps out their fields of interest (e.g., Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Web Development), technical skills, and professional habits (such as preferred study hours and productivity traits)[cite: 1].  

The system transforms this data into numerical vectors and processes it through an intelligent recommendation engine that:
*   **Measures intellectual and skill compatibility** using *Cosine Similarity*.
*   **Evaluates the alignment of work habits** using a modified *Euclidean Distance*.
*   **Merges both metrics** into a *Hybrid Compatibility Score* to rank and suggest the most suitable teammates.

Additionally, the platform features an intuitive invitation management system, live student availability tracking (*Available / In a Team*), and an integrated chat system to facilitate seamless initial communication between potential teammates[cite: 1].

---

## 🛠️ Key Features

*   **⚡ Hybrid Recommendation Engine**
    Delivers instant, intelligent teammate suggestions with a high-performance response time of less than 0.04 seconds[cite: 1].
*   **👤 Profile & Questionnaire Management**
    Allows students to showcase their technical skills, soft skills, and specify their preferred project roles (e.g., Developer, Designer, Researcher)[cite: 1].
*   **✉️ Smart Invitation System**
    Manages team joining requests, enabling students to send, accept, or decline invitations efficiently while preventing chaotic team structures[cite: 1].
*   **💬 Integrated Chat System**
    Provides a built-in communication channel for students to coordinate and discuss project ideas before finalized team commitments[cite: 1].
*   **📊 Admin Dashboard**
    Empowers college administrators and supervisors to monitor platform activity, track student statistics, and oversee fully formed teams.
